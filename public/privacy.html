Privacy Policy

Last updated: June 30, 2026

FIFA 2026 Tracker ("the app") is a personal project that displays live World Cup match data and optionally syncs matches to your Google Calendar. This policy explains what data is collected and how it is used.

Data we collect

The app does not collect, store, or transmit any personal data to its own servers. All data stays in your browser.

  • Google OAuth token — if you connect Google Calendar, an OAuth access token is stored in your browser's localStorage solely to make Calendar API calls on your behalf. It is never sent to our servers.
  • Preferences — your tracked countries, theme preference, and API keys are stored in localStorage and never leave your device.

Google Calendar access

If you choose to sync matches, the app requests the https://www.googleapis.com/auth/calendar.events scope to create and delete events in a dedicated "FIFA World Cup 2026" calendar it creates on your behalf. The app only ever reads or modifies that specific calendar and will never access any other calendar data.

You can revoke access at any time via Google Account Permissions.

Third-party data

Live match data is fetched directly from the ESPN public API in your browser. No personal data is included in those requests.
